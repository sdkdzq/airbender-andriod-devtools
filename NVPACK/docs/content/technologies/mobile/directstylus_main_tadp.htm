DirectStylus

Introduction

Developing applications that take advantage of DirectStylus technology is relatively simple. By relying on standard Android APIs and mechanisms to report stylus events, NVIDIA minimizes the amount of custom development work you need to do and increases compatibility with other Android systems.

This document describes the APIs, mechanisms, and processes that you need to understand to properly optimize your application for DirectStylus.
