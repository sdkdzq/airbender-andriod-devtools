Unpacking the Dalmore Board

Installing the NVIDIA AndroidWorks software can take some time, due to the fact that the installer must download numerous components from the Android servers and from NVIDIA servers. Thus, it is a good idea to begin the install of the AndroidWorks and let it run while you unpack and assemble the Dalmore board.
