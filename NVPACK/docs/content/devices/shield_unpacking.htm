Unpacking the NVIDIA SHIELD

Installing the NVIDIA AndroidWorks software can take some time, owing to the fact that the installer must download numerous components from both the Android and NVIDIA servers. Thus, it is a good idea to begin the installation of AndroidWorks and let it run while you unpack your SHIELD device, and complete reading this document.
